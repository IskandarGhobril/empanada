## Do Beliefs Stick?

Levels alone donâ€™t tell the full story.  
What matters is **how sentiment changes from week to week**.

We assign each week to its **dominant sentiment state**
and construct a **transition matrix**.

### ðŸ“Š Sentiment Transition Matrix

> **Plot goes here:**  
> Heatmap showing transitions between Bullish / Neutral / Bearish states

The results show strong persistence:
- Bullish remains bullish **77%** of the time
- Neutral remains neutral **59%**
- Bearish remains bearish **64%**

However, pessimism is **less sticky** than optimism.
Bearish states are more likely to flip directly to bullish than the reverse.

This asymmetry suggests that **negative beliefs unwind faster than positive ones**.
